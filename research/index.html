The Science

Force Field Analysis meets AI

Priorify's process is rooted in Kurt Lewin's Force Field Analysis — the foundational framework for understanding organizational change.

Our process identifies, prioritizes, and targets the critical resisting forces. When focused remedial action weakens these barriers, the driving forces naturally move the organization forward.

The Strategic Event Value (SEV) algorithm is the mathematical engine. Unlike simple averaging, it calculates Normalized Ordinal Probabilities that account for systemic interactions among all issues simultaneously.
