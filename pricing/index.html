Pricing Rationale

Each tier creates natural demand for the next

Priorify Pulse® — Why free?

Pulse gives prospects a tangible taste of the methodology. It's genuinely useful — not a gated demo. But it surfaces complexity that a single person's perspective can't resolve, naturally pulling engaged users toward stakeholder-powered tiers.

Priorify Pro® — Why $2,500–$7,500?

Mid-market companies and department leaders need strategic prioritization but can't justify $75K+. At this price, you're competing with the cost of a single off-site retreat — while delivering more rigorous, data-driven output.

PriorityPath® — Why $75K–$175K?

This includes every AI enhancement plus confidential interviews, facilitated alignment, and the paradigm-shift moment. The optional Living Dashboard creates recurring revenue at $18K–$36K/year.
