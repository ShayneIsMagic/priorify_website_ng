The PriorityPath® Process

Five components.
One clear path forward.

Stakeholder Intelligence + Data Prioritization Algorithm + AI Accelerators = High-Trust Strategy.

Each step is AI-enhanced for speed and depth, but human-led where trust and insight matter most.

In practice

What diagram validation looks like

During Stakeholder Intelligence and Diagram Validation, analysts and leaders work from live views like these—not static slides.

Step 1 AI Intelligence Brief

Executive Alignment

Leadership, guided by a Priorify analyst, defines the project challenge and identifies key stakeholders.

How AI enhances this step

Before the session, our AI generates a Pre-Engagement Intelligence Brief — researching your industry, competitors, public sentiment, and organizational context. Executives walk in informed and ready to frame the mission with precision. The AI also suggests a draft stakeholder map to ensure diverse and critical voices are included.

What remains irreplaceably human

Navigating executive politics, building trust, facilitating difficult framing conversations, ensuring the right stakeholders are selected.

Step 2 AI Transcription & Synthesis

Stakeholder Intelligence

Confidential 1-on-1 interviews capture what your people really think — anonymously and without fear of reprisal.

How AI enhances this step

With consent, AI transcribes interviews in real-time and tags emerging themes. Post-interview, an automated synthesis engine clusters all insights into thematic categories with supporting quotes — reducing weeks of manual work to days. Pre-interview, AI generates role-specific follow-up questions so the analyst is perfectly prepared.

What remains irreplaceably human

Building psychological safety, reading body language, following unexpected threads, creating the trust that makes people say what they really think.

Step 3 AI Diagram Generation

Diagram Validation

Stakeholder feedback is organized into a cause-and-effect Validation Diagram showing interconnected issues and dependencies.

How AI enhances this step

AI auto-generates a first-draft diagram from interview data, proposing causal links based on semantic relationships and co-occurrence patterns. The analyst refines this draft. Stakeholders validate through an interactive web platform — zooming, filtering, commenting, and approving asynchronously.

What remains irreplaceably human

Refining causal relationships that require contextual judgment, facilitating validation discussions, ensuring stakeholder ownership of the final diagram.

Step 4 AI Scenario Simulation

Prioritization Analytics

Stakeholders anonymously rate each issue. Our SEV algorithm calculates integrated priorities that account for systemic interactions.

How AI enhances this step

AI enhances the rating experience with adaptive ordering and context cards that reduce fatigue. After ratings, scenario simulation lets leaders ask “what if?” questions. The AI Disagreement Analyst identifies where stakeholder groups diverge and hypothesizes why — generating discussion guides for alignment conversations.

What remains irreplaceably human

The validated, proprietary SEV algorithm. Facilitating the cross-group discussion where paradigm shifts occur.

Step 5 AI Living Dashboard

Your PriorityPath®

A clear, data-driven, stakeholder-validated roadmap. Remediation teams form. Action plans deploy.

How AI enhances this step

A Living Dashboard tracks progress with project management integrations and AI-generated progress narratives. Automatic stall detection prevents initiatives from quietly dying. Monthly AI reports keep leadership informed and trigger re-engagement when priorities shift.

What remains irreplaceably human

The facilitated alignment discussion — the paradigm-shift moment. Remediation team formation. The human guidance that turns a roadmap into action.
